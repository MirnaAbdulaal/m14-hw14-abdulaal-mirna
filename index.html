Hobbies

  • My first hobby is creative writing. I've always loved the art of turning our real experiences into stories that can be shared to many readers that can resonate and feel emotionally connected to the stories.
  • My second hobby is photography and fashion. I love the art of visuals and experimenting with colour and effects, which can portray different styles of photography.

Interests

  • My main interests are politics, literature and art. I try to incorporate all of these interests together in my work as I blend the art of writing, with knowledge of politics and the art of design to create impactful magazines.

Career Goals

  • One of my main career goals is to use communications to amplify the voices of those that are marginalized and are unheard, such as farmers, workers and minorities.
